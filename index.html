What You Can Do with Oh! Japan

Access Important Work & Life Documents

Foreign workers can easily download company policies, employment contracts, HR manuals, and multilingual guides on living in Japan.

Submit Leave Requests

From paid leave and shift changes, employees can handle essential requests through the platform—anytime, from anywhere.

Start a Consultation

Need help or guidance? Employees can confidentially start a consultation with HR, a trusted community support partner, or a multilingual advisor—directly through the platform.
