3. Service Description

United Ways Japan Inc. is dedicated to helping foreigners thrive in Japan by offering a comprehensive range of services that support both individuals and organizations.

For foreign residents, we provide:

  • Information & Guidance: Practical resources and expert advice on daily life, work, and culture in Japan.

  • Career Support: Job matching, career development tools, and opportunities tailored to foreign talent.

  • Consultations: Personalized support and sessions covering topics such as legal rights, labor laws, salary and compensation, language and communication challenges, and general workplace concerns.

  • Community Engagement: Networking initiatives that connect foreigners with supportive communities and organizations.

For companies and employers, we offer:

  • Business Solutions: Tools and services that help organizations effectively manage, support, and retain foreign employees.

  • Consulting & Training: Guidance on diversity, inclusion, and HR strategies tailored to a multicultural workforce.

  • Communication Tools: Resources that improve understanding and collaboration between Japanese companies and foreign staff.

Our mission is to create a more inclusive, supportive, and successful environment for foreigners in Japan and the companies that work with them.
