Privacy Policy

This policy is effective as of 17th June 2025

1. Introduction

United Ways Japan Inc. is committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our website, services, or communication platforms.

By using our website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Personal Information We Collect

2.1. Information you provide to us

When you interact with our services, you may voluntarily provide us with personal information, including:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number

  • Contact details submitted via forms or surveys

  • Messages, inquiries, or feedback you send us

  • Information related to event participation, donations, or applications

2.2. Automatic data collection

When you access our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address, browser type, device information

  • Pages visited, time spent on the site, and referral source

  • Cookies and other tracking technologies for analytics or functionality

2.3. User comments and content

If you post comments, reviews, or submit content through our platform (e.g., event feedback or discussion forums), we may store that information along with your display name and timestamp.

2.4. Information from other sources

We may receive information about you from third-party platforms (such as event partners, social media, or public databases) to improve our services or verify information accuracy.

3. Types of Information

The types of data we process include:

  • Identifying Information (e.g., name, email)

  • Technical Data (e.g., browser/device/IP)

  • Communication Data (e.g., messages, feedback)

  • Usage Data (e.g., interactions with our website or services)

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial, biometric, or health information unless explicitly required and consented to.

4. How We Use Personal Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support

  • Send updates, newsletters, or marketing content (with your consent)

  • Improve our website functionality and user experience

  • Monitor and analyze site usage trends

  • Manage events, programs, or donations

4.1. In mails and contact forms

Your data may be used to reply to contact form submissions or support requests. We keep communications confidential and do not share email content without permission.

5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers assisting in operations (e.g., hosting, analytics)

  • Legal or regulatory bodies if required by law

  • Event or program partners, only with your consent

All third parties are bound by confidentiality and data protection agreements.

6. Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure socket layer (SSL) encryption

  • Access controls and regular security audits

  • Data minimization and retention practices

  • Despite these measures, no online system is 100% secure. We encourage users to take precautions in sharing sensitive information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the revised version will be posted on this page with the updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.
